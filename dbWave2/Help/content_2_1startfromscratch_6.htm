Start recording

The acquisition will start immediately after exiting the file saving dialog box or not, depending of your trigger selection. During the acquisition, the screen is continuously updated (or not if you have deselected the corresponding option). You cant hit the "stop" button at any time to interrupt the data acquisition. You can also change the vertical display resolution on the fly, by hitting the upper right button (in the shape of a magnifying glass) and moving the vertical scrollbar. If you hit the button just underneath the magnifying glass button, then moving the scrollbar will move the curve up or down (change the position of 0 mV).

On the central screen, you see here a white stripe, while actually, on a real screen, the entire screen is white. This is due to the capture screen program which enabled us to take a snapshot. What you see here is the display of 2 of the 10 acquisition buffers defined previously.

At the end of the acquisition bout, dbWave displays a dialog box asking you to validate or not the file. If you hit yes, or do nothing during 4 s, data are added to the database.

The upper panel is updated with the information you have entered for the database (and into the data file):

And the upper row of icons now let you access to the different sections of the program: "Database", "Data" and "Detect".

You should now hit "Database".
